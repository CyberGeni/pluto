Let us be your mouths and ears.

Pluto is a free, easy to use, translation software for people who are hearing impaired. It translates text to speech, and speech to text in real time so that you can communicate with others better.

Speech recognition made easy

Pluto's interface is designed with simplicity in mind, allowing new users to tap into powerful translation features with minimal frustration or effort required on their part.

Convenient and Accessible

Don't let your disability stop you from communicating with others. Pluto helps people who are deaf or hard of hearing to navigate their daily lives.

Focus on what matters

We designed Pluto with you in mind. Don't worry about poor audio quality or being misunderstood - We will take care of everything so you can focus on the conversation.
